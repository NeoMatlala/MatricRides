How matric rides works

1 Find the perfect car

Enter a location and date and browse thousands of cars shared by local hosts.

2 Book the car you desire

Got the car you're looking for? Book online and the host will contact you withing 24 hours to further discuss pickup or delivery arrangements.

3 Enjoy your matric ride

Have the car delivered or pick it up from your host. Grab the keys, and hit the road!
